ABOUT ME

Hello there dear customer! My name is Lana Knott and I am a creative graphic designer with innovative ideas and a unique approach to artworks. I specialise in creating beautiful, usable and professional digital artworks. With more than 8 years in design as a freelancer, my ideas have helped build numerous brands and create beautiful artworks for different events. I am also a passionate developer who loves developing video games and creating unique gameplay experiences for various audiences. I love experiencing with new technology and producing games that create new communities and bring people together from all parts of the world.

This website is my personal portfolio where I display my qualities, studies, as well as my work and projects that I enjoy most. As far as my studying goes, I have graduated with honors Rhode Island School of Design (RISD) in 2016. I have also been awarded “Designer Of the Year” in the Digital Design Awards 2019, where I had the opportunity to learn a lot of new things and meet a dozen of talented designers.

I love what I do and I put a lot of effort and passion into the product I'm working on, as well as being committed to delivering top results on time and within the budget. Please make sure you study my portfolio and contact me whenever you make up your mind so we can come up with wonderful projects together!
